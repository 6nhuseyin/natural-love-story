Health Benefits

Juniper trees produce seed cones named juniper berries. These small berries generally are deep blue. Juniper berries have been used for culinary and medicinal purposes since ancient times. Berries of this valuable tree are used as a spice or flavouring agent with their woody or spicy aroma. But keep in mind that some types of junipers are not edible. Current research suggests that they may offer various health benefits. Here are 5 health benefits of juniper berries:

  1. High in nutrients and powerful plant compounds
  2. Provide anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects
  3. May have antidiabetic properties
  4. Could promote heart health
  5. Antibacterial and antifungal activity
